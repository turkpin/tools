Draw.io Text Extractor

Paste your encoded XML from draw.io shapes to extract formatted text

How to use:

  1. In draw.io, select your shape (list or rectangle)
  2. Copy the shape (Ctrl+C or Cmd+C)
  3. Paste the encoded XML into the input box above
  4. Click "Extract Text" to see the formatted output
  5. List items will be indented under their parent title
